Key Concepts

Line of Scrimmage

The line of scrimmage marks where the ball is placed before each snap and separates the offense from the defense.

Red Zone

The red zone is the area inside the opponent’s 20-yard line where teams have a strong chance to score.

Turnovers

A turnover happens when the defense gains possession through a fumble recovery or an interception.

Personnel Packages

Personnel groups describe how many running backs, tight ends, and receivers are on the field for one play.

Coverage

Coverage explains how defenders guard receivers, whether through man-to-man defense or zone schemes.

Field Position

Field position affects strategy because starting closer to the opponent’s end zone usually makes scoring easier.
