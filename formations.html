Formations & Positions

Offensive Positions

  • QB: Leads the offense and handles the ball on most plays.
  • RB: Runs the ball and helps with blocking.
  • WR: Catches passes and stretches the field.
  • TE: Blocks and catches passes.
  • OL: Protects the quarterback and opens running lanes.

Common Offensive Formations

  • Shotgun
  • I-Formation
  • Singleback
  • Trips and Spread
  • Empty Backfield

Defensive Positions

  • DL: Defensive linemen pressure the quarterback and stop runs.
  • LB: Linebackers support both run defense and pass coverage.
  • CB: Cornerbacks cover wide receivers.
  • S: Safeties help deep in coverage and against big plays.

Common Defensive Looks

  • 4-3 Front
  • 3-4 Front
  • Nickel Package
  • Dime Package
  • Cover 2, Cover 3, and Cover 4
