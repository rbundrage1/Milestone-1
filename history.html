History of American Football

Early Development

American football developed from older rugby and soccer-style games played in colleges during the 1800s. Over time, new rules made the game more structured and more recognizable as modern football.

Rule Changes

Important changes such as the line of scrimmage, the down system, and the forward pass helped shape the sport. These changes made the game faster, more strategic, and safer than early versions.

Growth of the NFL

Professional football became more organized in the early 1900s and eventually grew into the National Football League. Today, the NFL is the most popular professional football league in the United States.
