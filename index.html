American Football 101

This website introduces the basics of American football for beginners. It explains the sport’s history, rules, team structure, and common football concepts in a simple way that is easy to follow.

Purpose: My goal is to turn research about football into a clean, organized website for my IT 3203 project.

Key Terms

  • Touchdown: A scoring play worth 6 points.
  • First Down: Earned by gaining 10 yards in four downs or fewer.
  • Quarterback: The offensive leader who directs plays and throws passes.
  • Line of Scrimmage: The spot where the ball is placed before each play.
  • Turnover: When possession changes because of a fumble or interception.
