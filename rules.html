Rules & Scoring

Downs & Distance

  • Each offense gets four downs to gain at least 10 yards.
  • Gaining 10 yards resets the count and gives the team a new first down.
  • On fourth down, teams often punt, try a field goal, or attempt to keep the drive going.

Game Clock

  • A game has four quarters.
  • Each team gets three timeouts per half.
  • The clock may stop for incomplete passes, penalties, timeouts, and plays near the sideline.
